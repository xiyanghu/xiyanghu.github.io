Generative Learning and Augmented Decision (GLAD) Lab

Welcome to our lab!😆

Join the GLAD Lab 🚀

I am looking for highly motivated students (Ph.D., Master’s, undergraduates, and interns) to join the Generative Learning and Augmented Decision (GLAD) Lab at Arizona State University. If you are passionate about Generative AI, LLM-powered agents, Trustworthy AI, Human-AI Collaboration, and Computational Social Science, I encourage you to apply!

Who Should Apply?

Successful candidates should have:
✅ Strong programming skills (preferably Python/C++; experience with deep learning framework like PyTorch)
✅ Mathematical foundation (e.g., probability, statistics, linear algebra, optimization)
✅ Background in machine learning, data mining, or AI
✅ Research experience (especially with publications at top ML conferences or interdisciplinary venues—this is a plus but not required)
✅ Self-motivation and curiosity—you will have the opportunity to lead research projects and publish in top venues!

Opportunities at the GLAD Lab

🎓 Ph.D. Students

Currently I can advise PhD students in the ASU Computer Information Systems Ph.D. program. Please apply to this program if you are interested. Admission decisions are made by the committee.

💻 Master’s Students (ASU)

For master students at ASU, before reaching out, please first take at least one of the machine learning courses (CSE 475 Foundations of Machine Learning, CSE 572 Data Mining, CSE 575 Statistical Machine Learning, CSE 569 Fundamentals of Statistical Learning and Pattern Recognition, CSE 476 Introduction to Natural Language Processing, CSE 576 Topics in Natural Language Processing).

👨‍🎓 Undergraduate Students (ASU)

I welcome highly motivated undergraduates at ASU who are interested in research. I typically recruit students from:
🔹 ASU’s FURI/SURI/GCSP programs
🔹 Hackathons and research competitions
🔹 Students with strong academic records in AI/ML coursework

🌍 Interns & Visiting Students

Exceptional self-funded visiting students and interns may be considered. Preference will be given to those with prior research experience (e.g., published papers or strong recommendations from researchers I know). If you are interested, please include your CV, research background, and publications (if any) in your email.

How to Apply?

🔹 Step 1: Check that your research interests align with ours (Generative AI, Trustworthy AI, LLMs, Human-AI collaboration, and Computational Social Science).
🔹 Step 2: Prepare your CV, transcripts, and any relevant research publications.
🔹 Step 3: If you are applying for a Ph.D., submit your ASU Ph.D. application and indicate your interest in working with me.
🔹 Step 4: (Optional) Send me an email with the subject “[Prospective Student] Your Name – Interest in GLAD Lab” including your CV, research background, and why you’re interested in the lab.

⚠️ Due to high email volume, I may not be able to respond to all inquiries. If your profile aligns well with our lab’s focus, I will reach out.

Looking forward to working with passionate and talented researchers! 🚀
